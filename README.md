✨ Virtual Library Website ✨

Welcome to your personalized reading hub. Logging in is effortless — just type your name, no password needed — and your library is saved automatically. Each name creates its own unique collection, remembered even after reload.

📚 Library Shelves & Categories  
Your library looks and feels like real bookshelves: arrange books straight, slanted, or landscape, each with custom colors. Start with four categories — Romance, Fantasy, Mystery, and Classics — complete with solid, stripe, and speckle swatches. Add new shelves and categories anytime to make the space truly yours.

📝 Connected Pages

Wishlist & Recommendations: Track books you want to read, half-read, or those recommended to and by you.

Reading Log: Keep a timeline of your reading journey.

Reviews: Write unlimited reviews per book, with an Enhance with AI button to polish drafts instantly.

Quotes: Save memorable lines from your reads.

Customize: Set default colors, fonts, and backup/restore your entire library.

📖 Seamless Book Actions  
Clicking any book opens quick shortcuts to log it, review it, or add a quote — tying all pages together into one smooth reading experience.
